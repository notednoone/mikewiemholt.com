About Me

I always found myself surrounded by the smartest people

My fascination with technology began in the 80's with a Commodore 64 and Vectrex. While initially captivated by games, my interest in technology deepened with time, and in 1995, at the age of 18, I moved to the Bay Area to work at Sun Microsystems. My father was a mechanical engineer there and let me know they were hiring for Desktop Manufacturing (no diploma needed!).

When I arrived, there was a buzz about the upcoming "Java" product. The manufacturing building displayed big purple signs eveywhere with the name and the coffee cup icon for the product that would release the next year. I was eventually moved to Server Manufacturing which I really enjoyed.

This early exposure at Sun ignited a drive in me that led to roles at Xerox and eventually Siebel Systems. I joined during their "Fastest Growing Company in America" (Fortune Magazine) period, working in IT and then Engineering - an incredible experience, and have not worked that hard since.

My interest in web design was sparked during my time at Siebel where I would regularly visit the massive web team operating the large and internation Siebel Systems websites. When I was not working, I was up every night learning Photoshop and HTML.

I left Siebel before the bubble burst, moving to San Luis Obispo and focusing on learning more web design and development. This journey culminated in 2005 with the co-founding of our company LIFTOFF Digital, where I currently serve as CEO and Owner. In 2020 my wife and I moved to Eugene Oregon, a return home for me.
